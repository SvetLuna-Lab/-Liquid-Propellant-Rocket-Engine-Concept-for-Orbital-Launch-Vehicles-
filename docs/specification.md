| Parameter                        | Value                     | Notes                                     |

| Thrust-to-Weight Ratio (T/W)     | ~80 N/kg                  | Target for high performance                |

## Notes  
- Higher Pc improves specific impulse and efficiency but demands advanced materials and cooling systems.  
- Expansion ratio depends on ambient pressure: higher in vacuum gives better exhaust expansion and Isp.  
- T/W optimisation achieved via lightweight materials, compact turbo-pump systems, and efficient plumbing.
